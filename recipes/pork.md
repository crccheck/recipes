# sous vide dongpo pork 東坡肉
 
+ pork belly
+ xiaoshing wine
+ soy sauce
+ star anise (broken)
+ ginger
+ garlic
+ rock candy 
+ green onion
* double bag because we're running the sous vide pretty hot
* 180-F for 10 hours

* separate the liquid
* refrigerate
* slice
* sear
* thicken the liquid with corn starch to make a sauce
